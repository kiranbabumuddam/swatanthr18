About the event
 
The event mainly focuses on bringing out the interest among the students
towards Indian freedom  struggle, the means they adopted and the struggles 
they faced during the freedom movement. We indeed very much focused to bring
out the quizzing spirit among the aspirants. The game consists of two rounds
1. prelims
2. finals
Prelims has questions related to freedom struggle, Indian constituent 
and also about various national leaders. 
There are 30 questions time given is 35 minutes. The exam will be in jar format.
Finals will have various rounds the are related to above mentioned topics in a 
creative way.

Instructions:
  • Mobile phones are strictly not allowed
  • Need to carry laptop and LAN cable for prelims
  • If there are any ties during selections tie breakers will play a key role
  • Need to carry their own pen and papers
Venue and Timings

11th august

Prelims be on 11.8.18(Saturday) at A2 ground floor at 5:00 pm

finals will be on 12.8.18(Sunday) at A2 ground floor from 4.00 pm
